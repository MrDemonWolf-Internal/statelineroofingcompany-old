Our Story

Discover Who We Are

Exceeding Expectations with Every Project


Our journey began with a passion for safeguarding homes and businesses in Beloit, WI, through top-tier roofing solutions. Backed by Greg Huebbe’s over a decade of experience overall, Stateline Roofing Company has established itself as a beacon of reliability and quality in the roofing industry. Catering to both commercial and residential clients, our expertise spans roof replacements, new constructions, storm damage repairs, and more.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to precision and transparent communication, ensuring every client feels informed and confident at every stage.

Our promise is not just to deliver exceptional roofing services but to enhance the safety and value of every property we touch, backed by our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction.
